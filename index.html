2) Allocation Rules (How we split the costs)

  1. Family restaurant: Each host pays the restaurant cost for their own family size.
  2. Guest restaurant: The restaurant cost attributable to 23 guests is split equally among hosts, except V2 is waived from this portion.
  3. Shared non-restaurant: Flowers + Priest gift + Pastry are split equally among hosts, except V2 is waived from this portion.
  4. V2 waiver: V2 pays only the restaurant cost for V2’s 5 people. V2’s waived portion is reallocated equally to V1, V3, and M. V4 stays unchanged.
  5. Rounding/pennies: V1 absorbs all rounding pennies so the totals add up exactly to the grand total.

3) Brief Calculation Notes (Simple explanation of the math)

  • Restaurant per-person rate: $2,988.40 ÷ 42 (used to assign restaurant cost to each person).
  • Restaurant guest portion: 23 guests × (per-person rate) → split among hosts, then adjusted for V2 waiver.
  • Shared costs: $300 + $300 + $100 = $700 → normally split 5 ways, then adjusted for V2 waiver.
  • V2 waived portion: V2 does not pay (guest share + shared costs). That waived amount is reallocated equally to V1, V3, and M.
