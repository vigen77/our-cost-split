2) Allocation Rules (How we split the costs)

  1. Restaurant – host party portion: Each host pays the restaurant cost for their own party size (family members + any special guests assigned to that host).
  2. Restaurant – general guest portion: The restaurant cost attributable to the 23 general guests is split equally among hosts, except V2 is waived from this portion.
  3. Shared non-restaurant costs: Flowers + Priest gift + Pastry are split equally among hosts, except V2 is waived from this portion.
  4. V2 waiver: V2 pays only the restaurant cost for V2’s party (5 people total: 4 family + 1 special guest). V2’s waived portion (general guest share + shared costs) is reallocated equally to V1, V3, and M. V4 remains unchanged.
  5. Rounding/pennies: V1 absorbs all rounding pennies so totals add up exactly to the grand total.
Note about “special guests”
Special guests are treated as part of the host’s party for restaurant purposes. Their restaurant cost is paid by that host (not included in the 23 general guests that are shared).

3) Brief Calculation Notes (Simple explanation of the math)

  • Restaurant per-person rate: $2,988.40 ÷ 42.
  • Host party restaurant totals: each host pays for their party size (family + special guests).
  • General guest restaurant total: 23 × (per-person rate), then adjusted for the V2 waiver.
  • Shared costs: $300 + $300 + $100 = $700, then adjusted for the V2 waiver.
